Gen II LED Hard Strip

Gen 2 LED Hard Strip is a new and improved energy efficient linkable strip, that provides a smooth, even lighting, eliminating any hot spots. Encased in an aluminum casing, the LED’s are cool to the touch and provide 120 degree beam spread. Gen 2 LED Hard Strips are available in four sizes and two color temperatures (WW-3000K, NW-4200K). Four mounting options provide a seamless even distribution of light delivering outstanding light output and low power consumption. Gen 2 LED Hard Strip is dimmable when powered by appropriate LED driver or dimming device.

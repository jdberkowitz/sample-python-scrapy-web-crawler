LED Tape Light

LEDs mounted to Flexible Printed Circuit Board with 4.3 watts per foot brightness. Minimal fixture profile of just 2.0mm (3/32") high by 10mm (12/32') wide. Can be field cut and reconnected every 4". Safe, Class 2 LED Drivers power up to 20 feet of product in a single run. Dimmable. Easy mounting with 3M tape pre-applied to back of fixture. Offered in six LED colors and RGB colour change for dramatic effects! Our extrusions are ideal for recessed or in cabinet applications.

Specifications:

Lumen Output 325 Lumens per foot - CW 165 Lumens per foot - CW

285 Lumens per foot - WW 145 Lumens per foot - WW
Lumen Laintenance 50,000 Hours 50,000 Hours

*CW = Cool White, NW= Neutral White, WW = Warm White
